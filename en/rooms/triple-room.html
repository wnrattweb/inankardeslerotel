Triple Room

In Triple Rooms, a wide area that will increase your accommodation comfort with carefully thought out details awaits you. The relaxing wooden natural decor of the rooms will allow you to be one-on-one with the nature of Uzungöl. The most authentic rooms of Uzungöl, which has an area of 32 square meters, will give you pleasant and unforgettable moments.

Triple Rooms are located on the 3rd floor.


Specifications

The rooms have wooden coverings and stone motifs. It has skylights and a large balcony and overlooks the "City and Forest".

One king and one queen bed

The triple rooms have a rain shower.

32 square meters / 345 square feet
