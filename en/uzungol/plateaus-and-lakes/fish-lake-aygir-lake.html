Fish & Aygir Lake

Fish & Aygir Lake


We will only introduce these two lakes as they are the largest lakes in the region of lakes and are the easiest to reach by car. However, we would like to point out that there are a total of 25 similar lakes in the lakes region, both larger and smaller . In winter, both of these lakes are completely ice. Even in the hottest times of summer, you may need to buy winter items .


Because of the stagnation of crater lakes, they are a true reflection paradise for photography lovers. There are natural trout living in both lakes.After passing Uzungöl , you can reach Demirkapı village, fish lake and stallion lake by following the Haldizen stream upwards.


Fish Lake Altitude : 2300 m


Aygir Lake Altituda : 2500 m
